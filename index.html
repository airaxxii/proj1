Wanna Ask Something?

What is NFT art?

The NFT (also known as non-fungible tokens) art is one of the most trending arts nowadays. Also, it is a digital artwork that enables the designers in terms of monetizing their works. Additionally, the artwork can only have one official owner at a time, and they're secured by innovative blockchain technology.

Can I cancel or edit an order after it has been placed?

The placed orders are processed as fast as possible. With that it depends on how many days you will cancel your order, but your order may not be able to cancel and edit once they are placed within 24 hours.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Technically, your order may be received in three (3) business days. Because our NFT art can be received within the internet, particularly by sending you an own original copy of a digital file.

What types of payments do you accept?

We accept payments through online banking, Paypal.

How do I track my order?

To track your order, go to our purchase order site and enter your Order ID and e-mail address that you have used to place the order. However, even if you do not track it, we will be sending a message to your account when your order has successfully arrived.

Do you accept donation requests?

Fortunately, we are considering donations. It is one of our objectives to attain donations and make it a charity, particularly for the environmental foundation.
